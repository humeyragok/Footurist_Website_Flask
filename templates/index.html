FOOTURIST

It is a startup established by OMU experts that offers innovative solutions in the field of foot health using artificial intelligence, sensor, and wearable rehabilitation technologies. It contributes to public health through personalized insole production and R&D activities.

The Secret to Successful Projects...

Innovation and Continuous Improvement

Innovative solutions and technologies are used in project processes. Additionally, continuous improvement of processes and products is ensured.

Customer Satisfaction

Meeting customer needs and expectations is a top priority for projects. The project is shaped according to customer feedback, with the goal of ensuring satisfaction.

Quality Management

Projects are carried out in accordance with high-quality standards. This ensures the reliability and performance of the product or service.

Sustainability

Environmental and social responsibilities are considered in projects. Sustainable resource use and environmentally friendly solutions are developed.
