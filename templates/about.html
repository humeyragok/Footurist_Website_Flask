Mission

We aim to develop advanced technology products in foot health, orthopedic rehabilitation, and prosthetic solutions by combining innovations in health and technology. Utilizing artificial intelligence, sensor technologies, and hemp-based materials, we provide personalized solutions. By transforming our clinical and academic expertise into projects that contribute to public health, we promote sustainable innovation in healthcare through our research, development, and educational activities.
